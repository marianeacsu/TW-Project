Elafonisi Beach

The main attraction of Elafonisi is the beautiful sandy beaches fringed with pink coral sand and the crystal clear waters of the sea and the lagoon.

As it is located at a remote corner of Crete you also get a sense of wilderness, being far from everything.

Blue Lagoon

The Blue Lagoon is one of the best beaches in Malta, situated between the island of Comino and the islet of Cominotto.

The crystal clear waters in Blue Lagoon reflect the blue sky with a lovely cyan as well as let you see the pure white sand bottom of this part of the Mediterranean Sea.

Victoria Beach

Situated on Spain’s so-called Costa de la Luz (Coast of Light), a region on the western side of the country’s southern tip, facing the Atlantic Ocean, Playa de la Victoria is the most famous urban beach in the Andalusian city of Cádiz.

In the scorching summer months, Playa de la Victoria’s bright blue, cool Atlantic water offers a welcome respite from the blistering heat that smothers the south of Spain.
